About me

First thing first, I studied general natural sciences, and my university projects were academic with mixed focus. I published in international science magazines. I had to learn quickly and be flexible all the time.
During my PhD I solved differential and eigenvalue equations numerically trying to figure out what will happen with the electron wave when applying external single pulses. Will it change the hydrogen atom itself? Will it leave the atom and how? We found formulas for electrons escaping the nucleus field depending on the field strength and electron initial energy level.
Looking at the data, process and analyze them was my usual task while I was studying natural science. Whether it were measured data, or data out of simulations, I grabed them and worked with these arrays of data.
Regarding programming, I have experience in HTML, CSS, Vanilla JavaScript, React, C# .NET for backend and Python for data analytics, Flask and JWT-extended library for token authentication. I am also familiar with SQL for database management, and have worked with MS EXCEL and touched Power BI for data visualization.
My experience includes building RESTful APIs with Flask, implementing authentication with JWT, and developing interactive web applications using React. I enjoy solving complex problems and continuously learning new technologies to improve my skills. I really look up to the web/mobile apps and think they are often handy. I use them daily and I would like to be part of the team that creates them.
